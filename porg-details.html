BSc Sustainable Business Management

The Bachelor's degree program in Sustainable Business Management at Swedish Open University is designed to provide students with a solid understanding of business management principles and practices, with a focus on integrating sustainability considerations into decision-making processes. It provides students with the knowledge and skills necessary to become successful business managers while also promoting sustainability and social responsibility.

Bsc International Business

The Bachelor's degree program in International Business at Swedish Open University is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the global business environment, with a focus on conducting business across borders and the skills needed to succeed in international business. Students in the program learn how to navigate cultural differences, identify global market opportunities, and develop strategies to compete in the global marketplace.

MBA-Master of Business Administration

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) program at Swedish Open University is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of business management and leadership skills with an emphasis on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making skills. The program is delivered entirely online, allowing students to study at their own pace and on their own schedule. MBA programs can be completed full-time or part-time and may offer various specializations or concentrations to meet the specific interests and career goals of students.

Msc in Business Digitalization

The Master's program in Business Digitalization at Swedish Open University is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of how digital technologies are transforming businesses and industries. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in both business principles and digital technology, provides knowledge and skills needed to effectively navigate and thrive in the digital business landscape and allowing them to bridge the gap between the two and develop effective digital strategies. The program is delivered entirely online, allowing students to study at their own pace and on their own schedule.

Ph.D in Business Administration

The Doctoral Program in Business Administration at Swedish Open University is a rigorous and comprehensive program designed for individuals seeking to pursue advanced research in business administration. The program provides a deep understanding of the latest theories and practices in the field of business administration and equips students with advanced research skills. Students will learn to conduct independent research, analyze data, and present findings in a compelling manner. The program typically takes four to six years to complete and is structured around coursework, independent research, and publication. The program is highly customizable, allowing students to specialize in areas such as Digital Transformation, sustainability, Responsible Management Education and Organizational Leadership.

A doctoral program in business administration with a focus on digitalization is an advanced academic program designed to prepare doctoral candidates for careers in research and academia, with a specialization in digital technologies and their impact on business and society.

A doctoral program in business administration with a focus on sustainability is an advanced academic program designed to equip doctoral candidates with the knowledge, skills, and tools to address the complex sustainability challenges facing businesses and society today.

A doctoral program in business administration with a focus on Organizational Leadership is an advanced academic program designed to equip doctoral candidates with advanced training in leadership theory and practice, preparing them for careers in academia, research, or industry.

A doctoral program in business administration with a focus on Responsible Management Education is a comprehensive program designed for professionals seeking to make significant contributions to the field of business administration with a specific focus on responsible management education.
